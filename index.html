05 — How it runs

From first call to operating workflow.

First

A short fit call

We listen. You tell us one task that's costing your team time. If it's something we know how to fix, we book the Audit. If it isn't, we tell you and point you somewhere better. No pressure. No slides.

Then

Audit

Time with your team. We sit with the people doing the work. Count the hours. Trace how the paperwork moves. You get a written report with build candidates and what they'd cost.

Next

Build, in flight

We connect to the systems you already use. We write the rules for the easy cases and the tricky ones. We test it against your actual work. You see what we're doing as it progresses. No surprises at the end.

Ongoing

Run, over time

The automation is live. Your team operates the work. We watch for issues, tune things up when your tools or processes change, and fix what breaks. The goal is to stay reliable in the background so your team can stop thinking about it.
